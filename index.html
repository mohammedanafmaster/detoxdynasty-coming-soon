What is DetoxDynasty?

DetoxDynasty is a startup that focuses on helping people, around the world, quit any of their bad or harmful addictions, in a natural & habitual manner. It provides its users with relevant information, techniques, and ways to deal with their addictions and gradually quit them with ease. It strictly forbids the use of substances, drugs, or any other harmful products to quit addictions.

What can we expect in future updates?

DetoxDynasty is still in ideation phase and only "Coming Soon" website is released as of now. But, our users can expect a super-active & accountable discord community, a user-friendly mobile application, and an informative website in the future updates. We are also planning to add a lot more features to our website and application laterwards.

How will these features help me?

Quitting addictions is not a quick and easy process. It requires a lot of patience, dedication, and support from peers. Our discord community will help you engage with like-minded people who will help you stay accountable and motivated throughout your journey. Our mobile application will help you track your progress and manage your recovery process. Our website will help you stay informed with relevant information on addictions, recovery techniques, and much more.

Is it trustworthy and safe?

Yes, it is completely trustworthy and safe. We do not share any of your personal information with anyone. We also do not store any of your personal information on our servers. We only use your personal information to provide you with a better experience on our website & application and do not use any of your personal information for any other purposes.

How should I begin my journey?

Jon our discord server by clicking on the Join our discord server button and be a part of our productive community. Once you join us, we will guide you on each step of your journey and also notify you about our future updates in the announcements channel.
